Is there a strange fishy smell in the air? If so, the golden retriever will be the first to identify it. His flair is highly developed; also he is an outstanding hunter-reporter. It also reports easily as it breathes and will jump vigorously into the water to fetch a dead or injured bird. His happiness is to bring back objects to his master.
Very intelligent, the golden retriever is easy to train. They are calm, kind and loyal dogs, all qualities that make them ideal for the family.

This dog is very energetic; he also needs a lot of exercise outside.
