The personality of the lionhead is particularly pleasant. He is gentle, calm, playful, affectionate and can cohabit without problem with children, provided of course that he is treated with respect and sensitivity. Relatively sociable for a dwarf rabbit, the lion head rabbit is however wary of strangers.
